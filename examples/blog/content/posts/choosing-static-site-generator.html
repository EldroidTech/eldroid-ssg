How to Choose the Right Static Site Generator

April 27, 2025

Understanding Static Site Generators

Static site generators (SSGs) are tools that generate static HTML websites from templates and content files. They offer numerous benefits including improved performance, security, and simplified hosting requirements.

Key Factors to Consider

1. Templating System

The templating system should be intuitive and flexible. Some SSGs use familiar template languages like Handlebars or Liquid, while others might use JSX or their own custom syntax.

2. Build Performance

For larger sites, build performance becomes crucial. Look for features like:

  • Incremental builds
  • Parallel processing
  • Efficient asset optimization

3. Plugin Ecosystem

A rich plugin ecosystem can save development time and extend functionality. Consider:

  • Available plugins for your needs
  • Ease of creating custom plugins
  • Community support and maintenance

Popular Options

Hugo

Written in Go, Hugo is known for its blazing-fast build times and extensive templating features.

Next.js

A React-based framework that supports static site generation along with other rendering strategies.

Eleventy

A simpler JavaScript-based SSG that's highly flexible and doesn't impose strong opinions.

Making Your Choice

Consider your specific needs:

  • Team's technical expertise
  • Project scale and complexity
  • Required build times
  • Deployment requirements

Remember that the best choice is often the one that aligns with your team's skills and project requirements rather than just following popular trends.
